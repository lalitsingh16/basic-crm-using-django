SaaS CRM – Product Overview
1. Problem Statement
Small sales teams struggle to:
•	Track and manage leads efficiently across multiple channels
•	Identify which leads are converting and why
•	Maintain consistent and timely follow-ups
•	Analyze sales pipeline performance for better decision-making

2. Target Users
Primary Users
1.	Sales Executive
2.	Sales Manager
Secondary Users
3. Small Business Owner / Admin

3. User Pain Points
Sales Executive (The Hunter)
•	Data Entry Fatigue: Spending ~30% of the workday manually updating spreadsheets instead of selling
•	Context Switching: Losing lead context due to fragmented communication across email, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn
•	Missed Quotas: Losing high-intent prospects because of missing or late follow-ups
Sales Manager (The Strategist)
•	Pipeline Blindness: Inaccurate revenue forecasting due to outdated or incomplete pipeline data
•	Performance Ambiguity: Difficulty identifying where individual team members are stuck in the sales funnel
•	Reporting Paralysis: Spending hours compiling manual reports instead of coaching and strategy
Small Business Owner / Admin (The Stakeholder)
•	Customer Churn: No centralized customer interaction history leading to poor retention
•	Tech Stack Bloat: Paying for multiple disconnected tools that don’t integrate

4. Product Vision
The goal of this product is to provide an intuitive, unified SaaS CRM that enables growing sales teams to centralize customer interactions, automate repetitive follow-ups, and maximize revenue through real-time pipeline visibility.
